Experience

Internship at LEAD - Leaders for Education, Activism and Development

During the last school year at SABA, I had the opportunity to engage in practical work as a graphic designer at LEAD, a non-governmental organization based in Skopje that focuses on educating students and young people. My responsibilities included creating a wide range of visual content such as posters, banners, invitations, social media posts and stories, as well as certificates. In addition to graphic design, I was also involved in video editing, which allowed me to further expand my creative skill set. I contributed to the design and writing of email campaigns, ensuring that the communication remained visually appealing and aligned with the organization’s branding. This experience not only helped me develop my technical abilities but also gave me valuable insight into working in a dynamic, youth-oriented NGO environment.

Reference

Figma Project - UX/UI Design for Mobile Application

For my final high school project, I researched UX/UI design for mobile apps and created a food delivery mobile app design. I studied UX/UI, graphic design and all the concepts included: typography, psychology of colors, the process of creating a design, the importance of wireframes, ect. I made all the designs in Figma, including wireframes, prototypes, and final screens. I focused on making the app easy to use with clear menus and a smooth ordering process. This project helped me improve my design skills and learn how to present my ideas clearly.
